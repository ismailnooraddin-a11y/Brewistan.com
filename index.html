How Brewistan works

A smart flow with almost no friction.

1

Customer scans the QR

The QR lives near the cashier. One quick scan opens the Brewistan mobile web experience for that café.

2

Account loads or gets created

First-time visitors sign up with name and phone. Returning customers sign in and see their saved cards.

3

Customer requests a stamp

A single tap sends a stamp request tied to the café and branch. The request waits for staff approval.

4

Barista approves from phone

Staff sees pending requests, approves the correct customer, and the loyalty card updates instantly.
